A Local's Perspective

Florence is full of different activites and things to do and see. When students study abroad they are immersed within the culture and become familar with the local surroundings. You learn where to buy the best cup of coffee, how to properly taste wine, what streets to avoid, and the best places to go out to at night.

Out of the hundreds of restuarants, bars, and shops within the city, there are a handful that are truely the best, in my personal opinion.
