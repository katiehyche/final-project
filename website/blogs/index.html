Getting Personal

Studying Abroad in Florence, Italy, or anywhere, is one opportunity that I think every student should take advantage of if possible. New friends, new experiences and memories that last are what you take with you when you leave. Although it can be scary and unnerving when you first set out on this journey, it is worth it in the long run. Students should put themselves out there and take chances because the abroad experince will only last so long. As soon as you leave you'll find yourself say "I want to go back!"

Recording your experinces is almost just as important as living them. Taking a journal on your study abroad trip is advised because it is a great keepsake for when you want to look back on your time abroad. Write anything, paste pictures and get into it because your trip will be something you want to remember for a long time.
