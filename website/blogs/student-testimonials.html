Student Testimonials

A total of 22 Manahattan College students were part of the Florence, Italy study abroad program. Read what a few students had to say about their experiences abroad.

Klyie, MC Class of 2016

"Studying abroad in Florence is one of the bets things I hav ever done. I am an art history major and I wanted to take the art class that was offered because Renaissance Art is one of my favorite genres. I learned about Renaissance Art the semster before and it was amazing to see the artworks in person. On this trip I met so many new people, some crazier than others, and by the end everyone felt like family. My favorite memories were the group dinners that we had almost everynight because we always sat with different people and food left me speechless every time. I'm pretty sure i gained like 5 pounds, but it was so worth it"

Bobby, MC Class of 2017

" I took a religion class and an Italian class while I was in Florence. Every day we went to Italian from 9am-12pm, then had a lunch break. At 2pm we met with our other class and since I took religion, we ofter did field trips to churches and monasteries. The best part of every class was meditating after lunch, sometimes for a whole hour."

Meghan, MC Class of 2016

"I wanted to study abroad because I like learning about different cultures. I also know a little bit of Italian and I want to improve my speaking skills, so I think going to Italy will force me to use what I already know."
