A Personal Diary

On my trip to Florence I took a journal with me and tried to write in it as much as possible. Random thoughts, activites, dinners, and more are all in there and I frequently look back at it. After I got home I added pictures, tickets, maps, and anything else I kept so that I could turn my journal into a scrapbook. I even added my art tests and some of my Italian homework. This journal follows my entire trip from the start to finish.

"It started in April when I had the idea of going to Argentina for the Fall 2014 semster. As soon as that turned into one big failure, I discovered the Venice Summer study abroad program and quickly applied. They had a to die for art class and I had always wanted to visit Venice. Then I found out it was merged with the Florence program because not enough people applied. So here I am, at JFK airport just about to leave for Florence, Italy." - 5/27/14

“Today was our first day in florence and it was a day off because it is a holiday that is equivalent to 4th of July. We met early this morning at the Duomo and took a bus up to Fiesole, which is in the hills above Florence. The views were amazing!” - 6/02/14

I wanted to write about everything from clases to roomate drama. When I look aback at my book now, I laugh and remeber all the crazy stuff that happened while I was abroad.

“We climbed to the top of the duomo today in art class! Soooo many stairs but worth the effort.” - 6/11/14

“Last day of Amalfi! Pompeii and Mt. Vesuvius! We went to the ruin of pompeii and I couldn’t have been more excited about it. It was so amazing even though the heat was killer.” - 6/15/14

“Kylie and I went shopping at the San Lorenzo market and ran into our friends. We all did our own things and then met up to leave. Tonight a group of us are going out to dinner to have steak; I’m craving it so bad. Evelyn looked up the place so I hope its good.” - 6/21/14

“Our last day of Italian was today and basically we sang songs all day in Italian. We finished our oral presentations and then Laura started playing songs. It was pretty entertaining even though I was fall asleep because I’m so sleep deprived.” - 6/27/14
