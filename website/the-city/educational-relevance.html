Educational Relevance

The city of Florence is home to many schools and universities with a wide variety of courses and topics. Many students choose to study abroad through their school's progrmas, but there are numerous opportunites for students to apply to outside programs. A few of the most populare study abroad programs are with:

  • CEA Study Abroad
  • API Study Abroad
  • Arcadia University
  • Panrimo
  • SAI Study Abroad

While studying in Florence, students have the chance to fullyimmerse themselves in the culture. A great way to start this is by learning the Italian language. Along with taking languages classes, students hsould consider courses in the following fields: studio art, art history, literature, religious studies, history, science, fashion and cuisine. The city of Florence has so much to offer and there is never a shortage of learning opportunites. Students are encouraged to use the city as classroom and take advantage of its resources.
