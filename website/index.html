Study Abroad - Florence, Italy

Ah, Firenze! A city of delightful warmth, stunning beauty, and electrifying activity built upon the history of medieval European trade, the Renaissance, and the romantic inspirations of artists, architects, and writers. Florence, Italy is a city that will delight the senses of students and provide a fascinating and inspiring context for study and exploration. It is a city that will forever live in the hearts of students who have lived and studied there.

Florence has been drawing students to its universities for centuries, thanks in part to the numerous well-known artists, architects, scientists, thinkers, and writers who have paved the way for future intellectuals.
